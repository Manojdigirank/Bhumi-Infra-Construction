About Us

Bhumi Infra Construction & Consultant is a trusted and professional construction company providing high-quality residential and commercial construction services. We specialize in new construction, renovation, and project consultancy with a focus on quality materials, skilled workmanship, and timely delivery.

Our mission is to build strong, reliable, and value-driven spaces with complete customer satisfaction. With years of experience in the construction industry, we have successfully completed numerous projects across Madhya Pradesh.

We believe in transparency, quality work, and building long-term relationships with our clients. Our team of experienced professionals ensures that every project meets the highest standards of construction excellence.
